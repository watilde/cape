# Mission, Vision & Values

## Mission
Enable stylish young adults to manage their daily tasks with aesthetic delight and effortless simplicity.

## Vision
Create the cutest task management experience that makes productivity feel like self-expression rather than a chore.

## Values

### 1. Aesthetic-First Design
Every pixel serves beauty. We believe that aesthetics aren't superficial—they're fundamental to user motivation. Our interface prioritizes visual harmony, delightful micro-interactions, and a cohesive cute design language that inspires users to engage with their tasks daily.

### 2. Simplicity Without Compromise
We strip away unnecessary complexity while preserving powerful functionality. Task management should feel intuitive and immediate, never overwhelming. Users accomplish more when the interface gets out of their way and lets them focus on what matters.

### 3. User Ownership Through LocalStorage
We respect user privacy and autonomy by storing all data locally on their device. No cloud dependency, no tracking, no surprise changes. Users own their data completely, building trust through transparency and control.

## Target Market

**Primary Users:** Young adults (ages 18-35) who prioritize aesthetic experiences and personal style in their digital tools.

**Core Need:** These users want to organize their daily tasks and goals but refuse to compromise on visual design. Existing task managers feel corporate, impersonal, and uninspiring. They seek a tool that aligns with their identity as style-conscious individuals while delivering genuine productivity improvements.

## Product Positioning

**Differentiators:**
- **Cute-First Approach:** Unlike generic task managers with utilitarian interfaces, ToDoアプリ leads with adorable design as the core product promise, not an afterthought
- **Local-First Architecture:** Full data ownership without cloud synchronization, appealing to privacy-conscious users and those skeptical of SaaS lock-in
- **Web-Based Native Performance:** Lightweight React application with LocalStorage delivers instant interactions and zero loading friction
- **TypeScript Foundation:** Type-safe, maintainable codebase ensures design quality translates to code quality

## Success Vision

### Short-Term (0-3 months)
Launch a fully functional MVP with core task CRUD operations, local persistence, and cohesive cute UI. Establish product-market fit with early adopter feedback. Target 500+ daily active users who actively recommend the app to friends.

### Medium-Term (3-9 months)
Expand feature set with task categories, priority levels, recurring tasks, and customizable cute themes. Build a small but passionate community through social sharing and user-generated content. Achieve 5,000+ monthly active users with high retention rates (40%+ monthly return).

### Long-Term (9+ months)
Become the go-to task manager for style-conscious young adults globally. Explore selective monetization through premium themes and optional features while keeping core functionality free. Maintain the cute aesthetic across all features without ever sacrificing usability.

## Status
**Current:** Version 1 in active development
**Platform:** Web (responsive design for desktop and mobile browsers)
**Philosophy:** Ship iteratively with real user feedback. Prioritize delightful execution over feature bloat. Maintain consistent cute aesthetic across every interaction.
